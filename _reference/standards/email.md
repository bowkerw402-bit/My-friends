# Email and outreach

## No dashes, ever (set 2026-07-18)
See `writing-style.md`. This matters most in email, where a dash is the clearest tell that a machine wrote it.

## Drafts only (standing)
Agents prepare email as drafts in the correct account. Will reviews and sends. Codex owns the drafting
because it has the Gmail access. See `handoffs/2026-07-18-claude-to-codex-outbound-email.md`.

## Sell the relief, not the technology (standing, 2026-07-16)
No AI words in any pitch. Name the chore the reader hates, and the fact it will be done for them.

## Zero input from the reader (standing, 2026-07-16)
The ask should be forwarding one document, or nothing. If the email asks them to gather things, it fails.
