## What stays alive while frozen (the whole list)

1. **The landing page** stays up; inbound enquiries get answered within a day. Inbound is the one unfreeze trigger that costs nothing.
2. **One warm vouching day, if and only if** a personal APS connection exists who would host a team day free/at-cost in exchange for being documented (photos, outcomes, a named quote). This is the proof asset everything later depends on. No connection = no day; do NOT manufacture one via cold outreach.
3. **One referral ask per month** — a single message to someone Will actually knows, nothing more.
4. **Quarterly IPAA attendance** if convenient — relationship maintenance, not pipeline work.

Everything else — census-plan processing, APSJobs monitoring, AAO checks, artefact building, target-list construction — is STOPPED. Zero weekly blocks. Zero cold outreach of any kind.

- A genuine inbound enquiry or referral arrives, or
- The vouching day happens and produces the proof asset, or

# APPENDIX — The v1.1 playbook (for unfreeze; unchanged)

## Operating rule
Contact only when ALL FIVE hold: strong account FIT + a CURRENT dated team-level event (≤120 days) + a NAMED accountable person + a LEGITIMATE route + a defensible public warrant. Account-level facts (a census plan, an old MoG change) never justify a personal approach by themselves.

## Procurement language — hard rules (Codex-corrected, permanent)
- BANNED: any claim that sub-A$10k means EL1 approval, less scrutiny, or avoidance of procurement process. Thresholds are not delegations. "Out of scrutiny" framing = advising spend-structuring = reputational poison.
- SAY: modest, clearly scoped packages with quote, agreement, insurance and delivery records "to support assessment through your agency's normal process."
- Substance over vocabulary: real agenda, named objectives, qualified facilitation, tangible outputs, proportionate catering. The words follow the substance.
- Artefacts are delivery evidence, clearly NON-ACCREDITED. No "certificates." Never claim reportability against a census plan — provide an internal-justification TEMPLATE the buyer adapts and approves.
- Never "retreat/away-day/reward/celebration." Use "structured capability day / team transition support / facilitated development session." Never imply dysfunction. Only cite public documents.
